Checkout

A quick checklist before you head out.

1 Load any remaining dirty dishes and start the dishwasher.
2 Remove all food items you brought with you.
3 Empty trash into the outdoor bear-proof bin.
4 Place used towels in the laundry area.
5 Lock all doors and windows before leaving.
6 Text us when you depart so we can alert the cleaning team.
