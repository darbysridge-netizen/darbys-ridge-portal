Cabin Guide

House Notes

  • Please lock doors when leaving.
  • Please use caution on decks, steps, and outdoor areas.
  • Follow hot tub rules before use.
  • Keep food secured and do not leave trash outside.

Helpful Info

  • Nearest grocery and restaurants are a short drive away.
  • Downtown Blue Ridge is nearby for shopping and dining.
  • Reach out through Airbnb with any questions during your stay.

Checkout

  • Check out time is by 10:00 AM unless otherwise arranged.
  • Please place used towels in the bathroom or laundry area.
  • Load and start the dishwasher if needed.
  • Please place trash in the proper bins.
  • Turn off lights, lock doors, and make sure windows are closed.
  • Message us through Airbnb once you’ve checked out if needed.
