Turnover Checklist

  • Make all beds and fluff pillows
  • Clean and reset bathrooms
  • Wipe kitchen counters and appliances
  • Check refrigerator and remove leftover items
  • Restock paper goods, soaps, and coffee items
  • Check hot tub cover and outdoor areas
  • Sweep entry and check decks
  • Take final walkthrough photos

Toilet paper, paper towels, trash bags, dish pods, hand soap, coffee supplies, laundry items, and guest welcome touches.
